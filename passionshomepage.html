Genshin Impact

Official Website

Genshin Impact is a fantasy relam where gods known as Archon's reign over Teyvat. These 7 Archon's bestow Visons which are like elemental powers upon those who have a certain ambition. These Archon's reign over Pyro, Cryo, Hydro, Anemo, Electro, Geo and Dendro. In the game there is a gatch system where you can try your luck to get characters who can use the elements to join your team.
